Common Questions

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call our 24/7 emergency line immediately. For tooth loss, keep the tooth moist in a container of milk and visit us within 60 minutes.

How can I access my medical reports?

Log in to the Patient Portal using your registered mobile number to view and download your digital X-rays and prescriptions.

Do you offer cashless insurance?

We partner with major health insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card during your visit for a pre-check.

Visit Guidelines
